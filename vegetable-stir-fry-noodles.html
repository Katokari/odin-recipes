Vegetable Stir-Fry Noodles

Description

Vegetable stir-fry noodles is a vibrant, healthy dish that combines tender noodles with a mix of colorful, crunchy vegetables in a savory sauce.

It's quick to prepare, full of flavor, and perfect for a wholesome meal.

Ingredients

  • 200g noodles (such as lo mein or rice noodles)
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp honey or sugar
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Sesame seeds and sliced green onions (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat vegetable oil over medium heat.
  3. Add garlic and saut√© until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Add the bell pepper, carrot, and broccoli. Stir-fry until vegetables are tender but still crisp, about 5 minutes.
  5. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, sesame oil, honey, and red pepper flakes.
  6. Add the cooked noodles and sauce mixture to the pan. Toss everything together until well coated.
  7. Garnish with sesame seeds and sliced green onions if desired.
  8. Serve warm and enjoy your vegetable stir-fry noodles!
